Time: 4/4
Tempo: quarter = 100 (lively)

C   C   C   D | E   E   D -  | E   F   G -  | C - - -  ||
G   G   G   E | C   C   C -  | G   G   G -  | C - - -  ||

Chords: | C | C | F | C |  (repeat)

| C - | C - | F - | C - |

## Practice tips
- Emphasize smooth, even rhythm for the "rowing" feel.
- Use legato (connect notes) on the melody.
- Sing the words as you play to lock in the melody.
